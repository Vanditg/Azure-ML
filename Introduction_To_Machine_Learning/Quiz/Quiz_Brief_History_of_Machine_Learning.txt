Question 1:
There is often confusion between the terms machine learning, deep learning, and artificial intelligence. See if you can match each term with its description:

Ans:
	Description										Term
	
- A broad term that refers to computers 			- Artificial Intelligence
  thinking more like humans.
- A subcategory of artificial intelligence 			- Machine Learning
  that involves learning from data without 
  being explicitly programmed.
- A subcategory of machine learning that 			- Deep Learning
  uses a layered neural-network architecture
  originally inspired by the human brain.
